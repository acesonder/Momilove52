# Momilove52
Mom I love









⸻

1. Core Patient & Caregiver Features
	•	Patient Profiles with demographics, health history, contact details, and profile photos.
	•	Caregiver Profiles with certifications, role, shift availability, and languages spoken.
	•	Emergency Contact Lists with click-to-call or click-to-text integration.
	•	Consent & Privacy Management (HIPAA/PIPEDA compliant) with signed forms stored digitally.
	•	Multi-patient Dashboard for caregivers managing multiple clients at once.
	•	Shared Care Notes between caregivers, family members, and medical professionals.
	•	Family Portal Access with view-only or limited editing rights.
	•	Role-based Permissions (patient, caregiver, admin, doctor, family member).
	•	Care Plan Templates for chronic illnesses, post-surgery care, and rehabilitation.
	•	Medication Management System (see section 4 below for deep dive).

⸻

2. Medical & Health Tracking Modules
	•	Vitals Tracking — blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate.
	•	Glucose Monitoring — manual input or device integration.
	•	Weight & BMI Logs with visual progress charts.
	•	Sleep Tracking — hours slept, sleep quality, wake times.
	•	Pain Level Logging — numerical or visual scale with body diagram annotation.
	•	Wound Care Tracking — photo upload, healing progress logs, dressing changes.
	•	Diet & Nutrition Tracking — meals, calories, macros, hydration logs.
	•	Exercise & Mobility Tracking — physio exercises, steps, activity level.
	•	Mood & Mental Health Logs — mood tracking scale, anxiety/depression self-assessments.
	•	Chronic Condition Modules — COPD, diabetes, heart failure tracking with condition-specific alerts.
	•	Menstrual & Reproductive Health Tracker (if relevant).
	•	Vaccination Records with reminders.
	•	Allergy Database with reaction severity and treatment notes.

⸻

3. Appointment & Scheduling Tools
	•	Calendar View with patient-specific appointments.
	•	Recurring Appointment Scheduling for long-term treatments.
	•	Telehealth Video Call Integration directly inside the app.
	•	Automated SMS/Email Reminders for appointments.
	•	Local Specialist Directory with booking options.
	•	Transportation Scheduling for patients without mobility.
	•	Waitlist Management for high-demand services.

⸻

4. Medication & Prescription Management
	•	Medication List with dosage, timing, prescribing doctor, and refill dates.
	•	Medication Reminders — push notification, SMS, or in-app alerts.
	•	Medication Interaction Checker for safety.
	•	Refill Request Button to alert pharmacy or doctor.
	•	Barcode Scanning to add medications by packaging.
	•	Medication History Log showing adherence percentage.
	•	PRN (As Needed) Medication Tracking with time limits and logs.

⸻

5. Communication & Collaboration Tools
	•	Secure Messaging System between patients, caregivers, doctors, and family.
	•	Voice-to-Text Notes for quick logging.
	•	Audio/Video Messaging for remote consultations.
	•	Group Chat Rooms for multi-caregiver teams.
	•	Emergency Alert Button that pings caregivers and 911 integration.
	•	Shift Handover Notes for seamless caregiver transitions.

⸻

6. Document & File Management
	•	Secure Document Upload — lab results, discharge summaries, care instructions.
	•	Automated File Tagging for quick searching.
	•	Printable Care Summaries for doctors.
	•	PDF Export of Logs & Reports for insurance or medical review.
	•	Version History Tracking for updated care plans.

⸻

7. UI/UX & Accessibility Features
	•	Dark Purple Theme with Customization — multiple shades and accent colors.
	•	Dynamic Font Size Adjuster for low-vision users.
	•	High-Contrast Mode toggle.
	•	Dyslexia-Friendly Font Option.
	•	Speech-to-Text Input for logging entries.
	•	Offline Mode with data sync when online.
	•	Drag-and-Drop Widgets for dashboard customization.
	•	Interactive Charts & Graphs with hover tooltips.
	•	Collapsible Panels to reduce clutter.
	•	Keyboard Shortcuts for power users.
	•	Touch-Friendly Buttons for tablet users.
	•	Loading Animations that fit the theme (e.g., heartbeat pulse).

⸻

8. Local Resource Integration (Milford, Saskatchewan)
	•	Local Hospital Directory with contact info and maps.
	•	Nearby Pharmacy Finder with hours and services.
	•	Emergency Service Numbers (including poison control).
	•	Community Health Programs listing.
	•	Home Care Services Directory with reviews.
	•	Local Support Groups for caregivers and patients.
	•	Transportation Assistance Services (paratransit info).
	•	Weather Alerts for extreme conditions affecting care.

⸻

9. Automation & AI Assistance
	•	Smart Symptom Checker to suggest possible next steps.
	•	Automated Care Plan Adjustments based on logged health data.
	•	AI-Powered Predictive Alerts for potential health declines.
	•	Natural Language Query Support — “Show me last month’s blood pressure logs.”
	•	Voice Assistant Integration (Alexa, Google Assistant) for hands-free logging.
	•	Auto-Summarized Reports for doctor visits.

⸻

10. Analytics, Reporting & Insights
	•	Trends Over Time graphs for each tracked health metric.
	•	Medication Adherence Reports with missed dose tracking.
	•	Caregiver Performance Reports for agencies.
	•	Hospitalization Risk Scores based on patterns.
	•	Printable Monthly Summary Reports for patients and family.
	•	Condition-Specific Dashboards (e.g., diabetes control metrics).
	•	Cost Tracking Reports for out-of-pocket and insurance-covered services.

⸻

11. Engagement, Education & Wellness
	•	Daily Wellness Tips based on patient condition.
	•	Interactive Health Education Modules.
	•	Mindfulness & Relaxation Audio library.
	•	Exercise Video Library tailored to ability.
	•	Local Event Calendar for seniors, support groups, and workshops.
	•	Goal Setting & Milestone Tracking for health objectives.
	•	Gamified Health Challenges to encourage participation.
